How it all started

Friend had challenged me to a 100 days of burpees of January 2026. Of course new year new me vibes. She is a great friend whom we worked together in the emergency department as nurses back in New York City. Her directions were to add 1 burpee for each day.

How do we play?

  1. First day would be 1 burpee.

  2. Add 1 burpee on subsequent day. For example, on day 13 we would add 1 more burpee making it 14.

  3. Good luck!
